Just a Darts Scorer Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Just a Darts Scorer is provided by Stocki. This Privacy Policy explains how the iOS app handles information.

Summary

  • No ads
  • No third-party trackers
  • No analytics SDKs
  • No user accounts
  • No sale of personal data
  • Most app data is stored locally on your device

Information the app uses

The app may store the following information locally on your device to provide its features:

  • player profiles
  • match history
  • tournament data
  • ongoing game state
  • app preferences such as theme and settings

Local multiplayer

If you use local multiplayer, the app uses Apple nearby networking technologies to connect participating devices directly. During multiplayer, devices may exchange player names, scores, assignments, gameplay events, and device display names needed for session discovery.

We do not operate servers for this feature and do not receive this data ourselves.

Camera access

The app may request camera access when you use the QR join flow for local multiplayer. Camera access is used only to scan a QR code. We do not upload or store camera footage ourselves for this purpose.

Sharing

If you choose to share match summaries, the app may generate text or images for iOS share actions. Sharing is always started by you. Once you share content through another service, that service handles the content under its own policies.

Data collection and control

We do not collect personal data from the app on our own servers, and the app is not used to track you across apps or websites.

You can control your data by deleting profiles, history, tournaments, or the app itself, and by choosing whether to use camera access or local multiplayer.

If you have questions about this policy, contact l.stockmann.10@gmail.com.
