Just a Darts Scorer

Play darts. Track everything. Skip the nonsense.

Just a Darts Scorer is a clean, feature-rich darts scoring app for iPhone. No ads, no subscription traps, and no clutter standing between you and the match.

Support Privacy Policy Legal

What you get

  • x01 formats like 301, 501, 701, and more
  • Cricket and multiple practice modes
  • Checkout help and match sharing
  • Player profiles with persistent stats
  • Match history, recent form, and head-to-head insights
  • Tournaments and local multiplayer support

Why this app exists

A darts app should help you score quickly and keep useful data, not push ads, upsells, or unnecessary friction. Just a Darts Scorer is built to stay out of the way when you need speed and go deeper when you want stats, rivalry tracking, and tournament flow.

  • Ad-free
  • One-time purchase
  • Fast scoring
  • Detailed stats
