Support

This page is the public support page for Just a Darts Scorer.

When contacting support through your preferred channel, include the app name and a short summary of the issue.

Helpful details to include

  • your iPhone model
  • your iOS version
  • what you were trying to do
  • what happened instead
  • screenshots if relevant

Common topics

  • questions about scoring or game modes
  • profile, history, or tournament data issues
  • local multiplayer connection questions
  • camera permission questions for QR joining
  • feature requests and usability feedback
